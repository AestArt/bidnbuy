REDEEMING A SUCCESSFUL BID

If you are successful in an auction, you will be required to pay the Final Auction Price, plus the listed shipping and handling charges within 48 hours of the close of the auction. This payment is required in order to redeem the item won and a failure or refusal to make the payment within the 48-hour timeframe will be considered a forfeiture of the auction win. Once a forfeiture occurs, the next runner-up bidder will be offered the item. The runner-up bidder will also have 48 hours from the time of notification within which to pay the Final Bid Purchase Price and the associated shipping and handling charges, in order to redeem the item. Each subsequent runner-up bidder will be offered the item in turn, until a bidder redeems it. If no bidder redeems the item, the item may be offered up again for auction.

AUCTION LIMITS

Generally, there are no win limits for items valued at under N10,000 (Ten Thousand Naira) and you may win an unlimited number of these items at any time on the Kobobid Site. However, you may only win up to a maximum of 3 (three) of such items within a 24-hour period.

  1. You may only win up to a maximum of 3 (three) items daily. This cannot be overridden by using a Limit Crusher.

  2. You may only win up to a maximum of 10 (ten) items within any 30-day period. This cannot be overridden by using a Limit Crusher.

  3. You may only win the bid on a maximum of 4 (four) items valued at over N100,000 (One Hundred Thousand Naira) within a 30-day period. You may, however, override this limitation with a Limit Crusher.

  4. You may only win the bid on 2 (two) items valued at over N250,000 (Two Hundred and Fifty Thousand Naira) within a 30-day period. You may override this limit with a Limit Crusher, 15 days after your last win in this category.

  5. You may only win the bid on 2 (two) item valued at over N500,000 (Five Hundred Thousand Naira)within a 180-day period. You may however, override this limitation with a Limit Crusher, 90 days after your last win in this category.

  6. You may only win the bid on 1 (one) item valued at over N1,000,000 (One Million Naira) within a one-year period. You may override this limit with a Limit Crusher 180 days after your last win in this category.
